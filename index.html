Core Capabilities in Every Application

While each industry app is unique, all share these foundational business management features

01

Customer & Property Management

Centralized customer database with complete service history, multiple property tracking, and comprehensive payment records. Industry-specific fields and relationships built into each application.

02

Job Scheduling & Dispatch

Branch-wise scheduling with real-time staff availability, workload visibility, and conflict prevention. Each industry's unique scheduling requirements and constraints are embedded in the system.

03

Field Staff Mobile Access

Complete mobile platform for field workers to access job details, view customer history, update status, add notes and photos, and capture signatures on-site with industry-specific mobile workflows.

04

Automated Invoicing

Instant invoice generation with SMS and email delivery. Support for industry-standard billing practices, payment methods, partial payments, and complete payment history tracking.

05

Multi-Branch Operations

Each branch manages its own staff, schedules, and inventory while connected to company-wide customer data and centralized reporting. Perfect for franchise and multi-location operations.

06

Inventory & Material Tracking

Track inventory at branch and technician levels. Record material usage per job with automatic low-stock alerts and ordering workflows tailored to each industry's inventory needs.

07

Operational Reports & Insights

Industry-specific KPIs and metrics that matter to your business. Clear visibility into revenue, performance, job completion rates, outstanding payments, and staff productivity.

08

Security & Access Control

Role-based permissions ensure staff access only relevant information. Complete activity logs and secure data storage with company-level isolation meeting industry compliance requirements.

09

Service History & Records

Permanent records of all jobs, customer interactions, service notes, and transactions maintained indefinitely for compliance, audit trails, and reference with industry-specific documentation requirements.
